Our Story

A Tradie Who
Gives a Damn

Johnny McLachlan started JBM Electrical with one goal: do good work and be straight with people. No inflated quotes, no cut corners. Just solid electrical work done properly.

Johnny's been in the trade his whole working life, starting out on the tools and working through every type of job going. Residential homes in the suburbs, commercial fit-outs, large industrial facilities. That experience shows in how the work gets done and what gets delivered.

"Whether it's a single power point or a full factory fit-out, the job gets the same attention. That's just how we work."

Based out of Point Cook, JBM works across Melbourne. Most of the business comes through referrals and returning clients, which is exactly how Johnny likes it.

Every job is backed by a 20-year workmanship guarantee and carried out to Australian Standards. You'll get a clear quote before work starts, full compliance certificates on completion, and a team that shows up when they say they will.
